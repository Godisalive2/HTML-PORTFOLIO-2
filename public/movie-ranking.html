Merlin

A two-part miniseries that retells the Arthurian legend from Merlin's perspective, focusing on his life before and during King Arthur's rise to power, including his involvement with Queen Mab and his role in the creation of Camelot.

One Piece

The One Piece movies, produced by Toei Animation, are theatrical films based on the popular manga series of the same name, often featuring original, stand-alone plots set around the contemporary story arc, with higher production values than the TV anime.

Blade

Blade" (1998) is a superhero action-horror film about a half-vampire, half-mortal man named Blade, who hunts and slays vampires to protect humanity, starring Wesley Snipes as the titular character.
